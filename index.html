The Women's Advocacy Project

The Women's Advocacy Project (WAP) was a ground-breaking, student-led research project at the University of Chicago Law School.

WAP followed the lead of studies at Harvard and Yale Law Schools in exploring and discussing the experiences, achievements, and challenges of female law students. WAP conducted student surveys, professor interviews, and classroom observations, and analyzed other publicly available student achievement data.

In May 2018, WAP produced a report on its findings, Speak Now: Women, Education, and Achievement at the University of Chicago Law School.
